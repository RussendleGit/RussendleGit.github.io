What are some key terms of Cybersecurity?

Threat Hacker

Person, group, or anyone that may raise a security risk to your company.

Security Compliance

Following legal rules for an organization to follow

Security Frameworks

Guidelines used for lowering risks.

Security Controls

Safeguards to reduce risks. Establishes good Security posture.

Security Posture

The ability to manage defenses, critical assets, and data.

Internal threat

Someone related to your organization, that can be a possible threat actor.

Network Security

Keeping a network secure, and preventing threat actors of taking advantage of it.

Cloud Security

Properly securing assets, limiting access to users that can access to remote users.

Programming

Set of instructions to complete repetitive tasks.

Quick Terms

Computer Virus

Malitous code to cause harm or disrupt a systems processes. Atatches to any file on your system and spreads across computers.

Malware

Malitous software designed to harm devices or networks.

The Brain Virus

In 1986 the Alve brothers created the “Brain Virus” to track illegal copies of medical software, and to stop piracy as a whole. With the consequence of any disk with pirated software on it, would be useless if a computer that would be infected uses it.

The Morris worm

In 1988 Robert Morris made the Morris virus. A program to estemate the size of the Internet, it crawled across computers infecting them to add them to a count to the size of the Internet. But an unitended bug apeared causing it to duplicate itself across the same computer, adding to the number of computers to preposterous amounts, also making those computers crash into an unuseable state with the low memory of the computers. It effected around six thousand computers which was 10% of the internet at the time.

The I Love You Virus

In 2000 the I Love You Virus, was created. (the certification calls is the “love letter virus” but that's not actualy the name) Making infected computers to send emails, it masked as an email from a freind as a love letter. Once opened, the Malware emailed of your contacts on your computer, this is a good example of a Worm. It also collected user info and passwords, this type of attack is, called a Social Engineering attack.
